About us


How we started

Before this year comes to a close, let’s take a trip down memory lane and discover how the MAGIC started…

More than three decades have past since your Magician waved its wand up high to cast a mystical spell that brought Share a Bear to life.

From then on, it has always been our best interest to uplift the art of gift-giving as part of the Filipino culture of celebration, warmth, generosity, and LOVE.

With 35 branches to date, Share a Bear continues to rage! Share a Bear forges on, creating extraordinary gifts to bring nothing but fond memories and overflowing happiness to many.

Time really does fly swiftly, and 2018 has been a glorious journey. We extend our sincere appreciation to you, who has walked with us through all these MAGICAL years.
